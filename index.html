Susan Gooding
I initially trained and worked as a Graphic Designer and Photographer. More recently, I have been studying computing and learning coding. I like to give back to the community through voluntary work and have a strong commitment to equality for women.

Friday 17 August 2018.

1. Coding

I find pair coding and discussing solutions in a group an energising and productive learning experience. I enjoy identifying innovative problems, devising a range of approaches and finding elegant solutions – combining creativity and systematic logic.

2. Education: Media Studies

As a volunteer consultant, I develop curriculum resources and maintain a blog to support photography and design for Media Studies. I train staff to support practical production coursework.

3. Photography

I was a Freelance Photographer for London College of Fashion (UAL). Previously, I had established a photographic studio, working as a Photographer and Art Editor for a publisher.

4. Giving back

Previously, I volunteered for a North Islington community centre. I developed their website, trained staff and developed documentation for their content management system.

5. Graphic Design

My initial career was in Graphic Design, designing books, magazines and other materials. I worked collaboratively and led groups as Senior Designer - managing workflow, organising photoshoots and meeting production goals.

6. Feminism

Additionally, during my early career, I volunteered at co-operatively-run magazines such as Spare Rib. For the Riot grrrl magazine, Bad Attitude, I contributed ideas and designed pages. I worked in design at City Limits and Everywoman.
