Hello, my name is Susan.

I initially trained and worked as a Graphic Designer and Photographer. More recently, I have been studying computing and learning coding. I like to give back to the community through voluntary work and have a strong commitment to equality for women.

Coding

I find pair coding and discussing solutions with others an energising and productive learning experience. I enjoy interesting problems, devising a range of approaches and finding the best solutions.

Education: Media Studies

As a volunteer consultant, I develop curriculum resources and maintain a blog to support photography, magazines and design for Media Studies. I train staff to support practical production coursework.

Photography

Alongside volunteering, I was a Freelance Photographer for London College of Fashion (UAL). Prior to this, I had established a photographic studio, working as Photographer and Art Director for a publisher.

Volunteering

Previously, I volunteered for a North Islington community centre. I redesigned and developed their website in WordPress, trained staff and developed documentation for their content management system.

Graphic Design / Studio work

I organised and directed photoshoots and became more involved in that area, which lead to becoming an in-house photographer.

Graphic Design

My initial career was in Graphic Design, designing or laying out books, magazines and other materials.

Equality
